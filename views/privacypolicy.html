Privacy Policy

Sash.Cash is committed to protecting all the information you share with us. We follow stringent procedures to help protect the confidentiality, security, and integrity of data stored on our systems. You specifically agree to the terms and conditions as mentioned under the head users information and privacy.

1. USER AGREEMENTS . Sash.Cash keeps track of your information to offer you the best possible rewarding experience. You may register with us by providing your email address, mobile number and other details, which allow us to provide you access to relevant activities every time you visit our Mobile app or Website. We may also ask you for information regarding your personal preferences and demographics that helps us to show you relevant tasks. Your information shall not be shared with any other entity, except our clients, who in a few cases may want your name and email ID with other data that has been furnished for them. We will use your bank account details only for reimbursement and payment purposes. We or our associate companies may also use your details to notify you about new services or special promotional programs, or send you offers or information if you have not opted-out. Emails / sms are sent only to Sash.Cash users who have not opted-out of such communication at any point of time. At any time, you can notify us that you wish to stop receiving the communication.

2. COOKIES . Like many other websites, apps, we may use “cookies” to improve your experience and to save you time. Cookies are little tags that we place onto your device. We assign a cookie to your device when you first visit us in order to enable us to recognize you each time you return. Through cookies we can customize our Mobile application or Website to your individual preferences in order to create a more personalized experience. Please note that the cookies we use for our website or email campaigns do not store personally identifiable information about you.

3. DATA LOGS . In an effort to make our mobile application or website as effective and enjoyable as possible; we collect certain information each time you visit us. We store these statistics in server logs. Once again, these statistics do not identify you personally, but provide us information regarding the type of user who is accessing our mobile application or website and certain browsing activities of that user. This data may include: the IP address, the type of browser, device id, push token, operating system, last visited time, how long the user accessed our website/mobile application in any given session, and the date and time of access. We may make extensive use of this data at an aggregated level in order to understand how our website or mobile application is being used. We may share some of the aggregate findings (not the specific data) with advertisers, sponsors, investors, strategic partners, and others in order to help grow our business.

4. SERVICE PROVIDERS. Sash.Cash may use various outside agencies (third party service providers) to make our mobile application or website operate. For example, we may use third parties to host, operate various features made available, send emails, analyze data, etc. Some of these third parties may need access to your information in order to make the services provided through our website or mobile application work. Information will only be disclosed to these service providers on a need-to-know basis, and they will only be permitted to use such information for the purpose of providing the particular services provided by such entities in connection with our mobile application or website.

5. LOCATION . Each time you launch the Sash.Cash mobile application to do a location based task, we collect your location information and may share this information to our clients to for authenticating completion of the assigned task. You perform a location based task then the client may get the specific location where you performed the Task.

Once uploaded and stored in with Sash.Cash, this content may be used by Sash.Cash or any of Sash.Cash’s business partners or clients. Use of this content may or may not be mentioned in the task description.
