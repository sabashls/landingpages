Benefits for Advertisers

Device Based Advertisement Hosting

Describes a target approach for customer who already own a specific device(mobile phone, tablet, PC). It can create a more specific marketing campaign, increase sales, and decrease the number of competitors in the market.

Geographic Based Promotion

A geographical target can be consumers in a city, state or country. Advertiser can select based on the fulfillment of the product. This would allow the team to break the market into sections by climate, density, market size, world or state.

Demographic Based Target

Advertiser can differentiate their audience based on a specific gender, age group, income level, or education level. It define the needs and preference match the product range of a brand.

Retargeting

Retargeting customized base on the interest, behavior of the specifically targeted audience. This enables the business to use its marketing resources more efficiently, resulting in more cost and time efficient marketing efforts .
